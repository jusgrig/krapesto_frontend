The restaurant is located on the edge of Dvarčionys, right next to a wellness pine forest that invites both relaxation and active leisure. The forest features outdoor fitness equipment, walking paths, and open spaces designed for enjoying fresh air and movement. Surrounded by nature, the restaurant offers a spacious and well-maintained outdoor area with a cozy terrace, a children's playground, and a large relaxation zone, making it ideal for families, gatherings, and celebrations. For your convenience, there is a large and easily accessible parking area right next to the restaurant, allowing you to park your car just steps from the entrance and enjoy a stress-free visit without worrying about parking. The restaurant has two floors. The ground floor features the kitchen, bar, and an authentic pizza-baking area where unique flavors come to life. The second floor offers a private space for your celebrations, gatherings, or activities, especially well suited for the colder seasons. Children are also thoughtfully accommodated on the second floor, where a dedicated kids' play corner has been created, allowing parents to relax and enjoy their event while little ones are entertained. When hosting celebrations on the second floor, you can make use of audio and video equipment, microphones, and other necessary technical facilities, ensuring a smooth experience for events, presentations, or entertainment. During the warmer months, celebrations and events can be organized both indoors and outdoors, offering a unique atmosphere that combines nature, comfort, and exceptional hospitality.
